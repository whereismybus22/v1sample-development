IOS You blocked location access !

Lets's get you back on track !

  • In the top-right corner of your browser, click on the three dots ( ⋮ )
  • Select Settings.
  • Scroll down and click on Site settings
  • Click on Location
  • Under the Blocked section, find and click on mlid-whereismybus.vercel.app.
  • Finally, click on the Allow button to grant location access.
